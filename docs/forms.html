Forms

Simple Form

You can active the form validate using stewed-validate="true"

Cooking

								<form class="form color-pink" stewed-validate="true" autocomplete="off">
									<div class="textfield">
										<input name="name" class="textfield__input"  max="10" min="3" type="text" required>
										<label class="textfield__label" for="name">First Name</label>
									</div>
									<div class="textfield">
										<input name="email" class="textfield__input" type="email" required>
										<label class="textfield__label" for="surname">Email</label>
									</div>
									<div class="textfield">
										<input name="phone" class="textfield__input" max="9" type="tel">
										<label class="textfield__label" for="phone">Phone</label>
									</div>
									<div class="textfield">
										<select name="option" class="textfield__input">
											<option value="">-- Choose --</option>
											<option value="1">Class 1</option>
											<option value="2">Class 2</option>
											<option value="3">Class 3</option>
											<option value="4">Class 4</option>
										</select>
										<label for="option" class="textfield__label--option"><span class="color-black">Choose your class</span></label>
									</div>
									<div class="textfield">
										<textarea name="mensagem" class="textfield__input" minlength="2" maxlength="300" rows="4"></textarea>
										<label class="textfield__label" for="mensagem">Message</label>
									</div>
									<button type="submit" class="button button--medium background-pink color-white">Submit Form</button>
								</form>

Disabled Form

Cooking

						<div class="textfield">
							<input name="phone" class="textfield__input" type="text" disabled>
							<label class="textfield__label" for="phone">Input Disabled</label>
						</div>
						<div class="textfield">
							<textarea name="mensagem" class="textfield__input" rows="2" disabled></textarea>
							<label class="textfield__label" for="mensagem">Message Disabled</label>
						</div>
						<div class="textfield">
							<select name="option" class="textfield__input" disabled>
								<option>Option Disabled</option>
							</select>
							<label for="option" class="textfield__label--option">Choose one option</label>
						</div>
